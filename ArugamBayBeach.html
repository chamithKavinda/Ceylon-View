ArugamBay Beach

Arugam Bay Beach is situated on the southeastern coast of Sri Lanka, in the town of Arugam Bay, which falls under the Pottuvil division of the Ampara district. It is nestled along the Indian Ocean shoreline, approximately 320 kilometers east of Colombo, the capital city of Sri Lanka.


Arugam Bay Beach is renowned worldwide for its

  • breathtaking natural beauty
  • vibrant atmosphere
  • exceptional surfing conditions
Stretching along several kilometers of golden coastline, the beach boasts pristine sands, crystal-clear azure waters, and a backdrop of lush greenery, creating an idyllic tropical paradise.

Nearby Attractions Lahugala National Park, Kumana National Park, Elephant Rock, Panama Tank, Muhudu Maha Viharaya
Accommodation Beachside resorts, guesthouses, hostels, and homestays catering to various budgets
Dining Options Beachside cafes, seafood restaurants, and local eateries serving a variety of cuisines
Transportation Accessible by road from major cities like Colombo and Kandy; local buses, taxis, and tuk-tuks available
